Discover UMD & Duluth

Why Elmo chose University of Minnesota Duluth and what makes this place special

Why Elmo Chose University of Minnesota Duluth

Excellence in Education

Elmo chose UMD because of its outstanding reputation for academic excellence and innovative teaching methods. The university's commitment to student success and hands-on learning aligns perfectly with Elmo's educational philosophy.

Beautiful Location

Located on the shores of Lake Superior, UMD offers a unique learning environment that combines natural beauty with academic rigor. The stunning campus provides inspiration for creativity and learning.

Diverse Community

UMD's diverse and inclusive community reflects Elmo's values of celebrating differences and promoting understanding among all people. This environment fosters growth and learning for everyone.

What Elmo Loves About Duluth

Natural Beauty

Duluth's breathtaking scenery, from the vastness of Lake Superior to the surrounding forests, provides endless inspiration and opportunities for outdoor learning and exploration.

Rich History

The city's rich maritime history and cultural heritage offer fascinating opportunities to learn about the past while building toward the future.

Community Spirit

Duluth's welcoming community and friendly atmosphere make it feel like home. The people here truly care about each other and work together to make the city a better place.

Duluth Area Highlights

Outdoor Adventures

From hiking the Superior Hiking Trail to kayaking on Lake Superior, Duluth offers endless opportunities for outdoor recreation and adventure.

Cultural Attractions

The city is home to world-class museums, theaters, and cultural institutions that provide rich opportunities for learning and entertainment.

Four Seasons of Beauty

Each season brings its own unique beauty to Duluth, from the vibrant colors of fall to the peaceful snow-covered landscapes of winter.
